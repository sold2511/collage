About us

At Good Nets, we believe in the power of sport to inspire, connect, and empower women of all ages and abilities. Our online directory is more than just a platform – it’s a community-driven space designed to make finding and joining local sports clubs a breeze.

Our Mission

Good Nets maps local women’s sports groups in one inclusive, searchable home. Beyond clubs, we offer coaching for those seeking to gain confidence and improve. We believe that sport is not about being the best or the fittest. It’s the joy of showing up as you are, supported by others who share your passion.

Our story

Good Nets was founded in Liverpool, born from a struggle to find women’s football clubs in my area. My search for a social, open-age team led to dead ends.

By a serendipitous moment, I found an inclusive team reviving my passion for the sport I’d left behind. As new friendships formed and my fitness returned, an idea sparked – what if finding your perfect club didn’t have to be a stroke of luck?

I imagined a solution to my struggle – a digital space where you could quickly and easily match your individual needs.
